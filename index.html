Our Story

Crafted by Tradition,
Refined by Time

Established in 1949, CV Cisarua has upheld a family tradition of leather craftsmanship across generations. From old-fashion tanning know-how to modern leather technology, we have grown into one of West Java's leading leather manufacturers — serving partners across Indonesia and around the world.

Situated in the heart of West Java, our facility maintains constant supply of quality raw materials from trusted local and international suppliers — a testament to our long-term relationships and our commitment to delivering the finest leather in the region.

How We Work

Our Leather Process

Every hide undergoes a meticulous, multi-stage transformation — guided by highly skilled technicians combining decades of expertise with modern leather technology.

01

Raw Material Sourcing

Quality hides sourced from reputable local and international suppliers in Indonesia, USA, Brazil, and Australia.

02

Wet-Blue Tanning

Chrome tanning process that preserves hides and prepares them for further processing and finishing stages.

03

Drum Dyeing

Colour penetration and crust development with a wide variety of tanning methods and shade selections.

04

Finishing

Modern finishing systems including spraying, embossing, perforating, ironing, and polishing — all supervised by expert technicians.

05

Quality Control

Stringent quality inspection at every step, ensuring each piece meets our high standards before reaching our partners.

Our Commitment

Environmental &
Social Responsibility

We are strongly committed to contributing to a better living environment — because we believe responsible manufacturing is not optional, it is essential.

Advanced Water Treatment

State-of-the-art water treatment facilities minimize environmental impact and ensure safe water discharge from our operations.

Green Scrap Upcycling
